ABOUT HEERAK TOOLS

Heerak tools is a rapidly developing startup founded by team of passionate, young and expert engineers in 2013. We manufacture Diamond tools such as Diamond Wire Rope, Mono blade segments, multi-blade segments, ceramic roller segment, squaring wheel segment, core bit and concrete core bit. Our conviction is to think out of the box and intend to design such breakthrough machines which can revolutionize diamond tool industry. We have garneredyears of experience in the field of Diamond tools and its allied Machinery. We also work for manufacturing of machinery i.e. Semi automatic fixed brazing machine, sintering press machine, mechanical cold machine, and Hydraulic cold machine, tumbler and mixer machine.The pioneer founder of Heerak tools is Mr Devender Sanadya who is young, zealous, hardworking and insightful engineer. He did master in Metallurgy and material engineering from MNIT (National Institute of Technology), Jaipur. His exemplary skill set consists of expertise in Powder metallurgy, Material Process, Research & development and Product Development.He is working on the path to generatin innovative ideas and accordinglyprovideapt solution to customer as well as manufacturer. Customer satisfaction and offering best quality tools are his top priorities.
